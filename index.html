The Augusta Rule

Section 280A of the IRS Tax Code

What is it?

The Augusta Rule allows you to rent your home to your business for up to 14 days per year tax-free. You don't have to report the rental income, and your business can deduct the rental expense.

How it works

1

Your business holds legitimate meetings or events at your home

2

You charge your business fair market rent for the space

3

Your business deducts the rent as an expense

4

You receive the rental income tax-free (up to 14 days)

Requirements

  • Legitimate business purpose for using the space
  • Fair market value rent (document with comparables)
  • Written rental agreement between you and your business
  • Meeting minutes or documentation of business use
Potential Savings

Up to $24,000

Based on 14 days at average fair market rental rates

This is general information only. Please consult with your tax professional to determine if this strategy is appropriate for your situation.
